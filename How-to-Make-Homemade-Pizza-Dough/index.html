How to Make Homemade Pizza Dough

Homemade pizza dough requires a few simple ingredients: flour, yeast, salt, sugar, olive oil, and water. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water and let it sit until frothy. In a large bowl, combine flour and salt, then add the yeast mixture and olive oil. Knead the dough until smooth and elastic. Let it rise in a warm place until doubled in size. Roll out the dough, add your favorite toppings, and bake in a preheated oven at a high temperature until the crust is golden and crispy.
