How to Make Homemade Pasta

Making homemade pasta is simpler than you might think. Combine flour and eggs in a bowl, mixing until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth and elastic. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes. Roll out the dough thinly and cut into your desired shape. Cook the pasta in boiling salted water for 2-3 minutes, until al dente. Serve with your favorite sauce.
